IMPORTANT: Not all of the examples in this folder have been tested for functionality. Use the samples at your own risk!

Event filter examples exist in 'PowerEvents\Samples\Event Filters'
Event consumer examples exist in 'PowerEvents\Samples\Event Consumers'

Event bindings exist in the root of the samples folder. This makes it easier to references filters and consumers.
The event binding scripts make use of the filter and consumer scripts, and together, are complete examples.
